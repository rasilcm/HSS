Welcome to HSS Mundur

Our Legacy of Excellence

Higher Secondary School Mundur has been a cornerstone of education in Palakkad, Kerala for over one century. We are committed to providing quality education that empowers students to excel academically and develop into responsible citizens.

Our curriculum is designed to nurture critical thinking, creativity, and character development, preparing students for the challenges of higher education and beyond.
