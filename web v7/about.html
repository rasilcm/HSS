About Our School

Discover the legacy, mission, and vision of Higher Secondary School Mundur

Our History

Higher Secondary School Mundur has long been at the forefront of educational excellence in the Palakkad district.

The light of knowledge ('Aksharadeepam') was first lit by Sri K.V. Sundarawaryar and Sri Kannath Achuthan Master. In 1933, the Mundoor Higher Elementary School was founded by the Mundoor Kizhakkewariyat family, with Sri Sivadasawaryar serving as the first manager.

In the 1940s, following the retirement of the previous administrator K.V. Achuthawaryar, the administration passed to Sri Sivadasawaryar. During this period, Sriman Sundarawaryar took over management, dedicating himself to the school's development.

A major milestone was reached in 1957 when the institution was upgraded to a High School. The inauguration on June 15, 1957, was presided over by Sriman P.S. Kesavan Namboothiri and inaugurated by P.T. Bhaskarapanikar, a key figure in educational reform. Following the passing of Shri Sundara Warrier in 1960 October 14, the school continued its journey, eventually being upgraded to a Higher Secondary School in 2010.

Our journey began with a vision to provide quality education to the youth of Mundur. Over the last century, we have expanded our facilities, introduced modern teaching methodologies, and built a sterling reputation for academic excellence.

Our Achievements

Over the decades, we have consistently produced outstanding results in academics, with our students securing top ranks in state board examinations and gaining admission to prestigious institutions across India.

Beyond academics, our students have excelled in sports, arts, and cultural activities at state and national levels. Our alumni have become successful professionals in various fields including engineering, medicine, arts, civil services, and entrepreneurship.

Our Philosophy

At HSS Mundur, we believe education is not just about imparting knowledge but about shaping character. Our values of integrity, respect, perseverance, and community service are integrated into every aspect of school life.

We strive to create an inclusive environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Our mission is to provide a transformative educational experience that empowers students to become critical thinkers, lifelong learners, and responsible global citizens.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality education that fosters intellectual growth, character development, and social responsibility, preparing students to excel in a dynamic global society.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence that nurtures innovative thinkers, compassionate leaders, and responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, respect, compassion, and lifelong learning form the foundation of everything we do at HSS Mundur.
